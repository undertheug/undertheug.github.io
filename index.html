About

From the depths

For the culture

We want to change the conversation on hip hop. We are creating the type of music that most inspires us, and are crazy enough to believe that relevancy need not exist at the bleeding edge. Quite possibly, as we traverse dusty paths outside the mainstream, we may find ourselves at frontiers unseen - those emerging from prophetic dreams and hopes of genuine heads who cherish what’s golden from old to new.

We are creating for ourselves, our loved ones and those who explore corridors often existing below the surface, deeply underground. But we are digging deeper still.

What exists in the depths? What exactly lies beneath, under-the-underground? Our name started as an inside joke, a poke at early creative attempts that seemed far off the grid. But we soon realized that this is the very place we exist for, the place we need to own. It’s not enough for us to be underground in the sense of nonconformity - we are digging deeper still.

We aim for truth, goodness and beauty. We write until we bleed, and then we keep on writing. Our soundscapes echo the rhythmic pulses of everyday life. We are unearthing creative expressions from the depths for the sake of those who uphold the highest standard. That is a story worth living into.

Under The Underground is our next chapter in that story.

Under The Underground

Music

Comprised of visionary artists survinylist and the G.I.B., Under The Underground is a force to be reckoned with in the hip-hop scene. Hailing from Finland, Under The Underground brings a fresh perspective to the underground rap scene, infusing their music with raw authenticity and thought-provoking lyricism. Their distinctive sound draws inspiration from the grit of the streets, the nuances of everyday life, and the pulsating rhythm of the urban environment. With a passion for storytelling and an unapologetic approach to their craft, Under The Underground creates music that resonates on a personal level while challenging societal norms. Their dynamic synergy and complementary styles seamlessly blend together, creating a sonic experience that's both captivating and unforgettable. Under The Underground's music is a journey through life's highs and lows, delivered through intricate verses and evocative beats. From introspective tracks that dive deep into the human experience to anthems that ignite the spirit, their discography is a testament to the power of underground hip-hop. Having gained a devoted following within the underground scene, Under The Underground continues to make waves with their electrifying live performances. Whether in small intimate venues or larger stages, their stage presence is magnetic, leaving audiences craving more.

Podcasts

Hosted by Aki Gibson (the G.I.B.) and Samy (survinylist), Below the Surface Radio takes listeners on a captivating journey through the vibrant world of hip-hop culture. From insightful interviews with renowned artists and producers to in-depth discussions about the latest trends and releases, our podcast is a one-stop source for anyone passionate about hip-hop. We delve into the stories behind the music, spotlighting the artists shaping the genre's evolution. Each episode brings a fresh perspective, engaging listeners with thought-provoking conversations, exclusive insights, and a diverse range of voices. Tune in to Below the Surface Radio for an immersive experience where hip-hop's past, present, and future come alive. Whether you're an ardent hip-hop enthusiast, an aspiring artist, or simply curious about the culture, our podcast offers an enriching blend of entertainment and education.
